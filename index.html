The 1954 Recipe

A Legacy in Every Jar

It started in a small courtyard in Nirmal, Telangana. My grandmother, Ammaji, believed that a meal without achar is like a day without sunshine. She spent her summers drying mangoes on the terrace and mixing spices by hand.

Today, Sri Pickles brings that same nostalgia to your dining table. No vinegar, no chemicals—just pure oil, salt, spices, and time.
